Murder of Doubt:
There are two storylines:
The first one plays in an old town in Holland and is surrealistic with horror murders.
The other one plays in Venice Beach and Santa Monica and is in the real world with a documentary style: Anna tries to get clear what happened on the evening before.

One of the psychedelic effects that slowly creeps in, is that most of the male voices in the film are the same: the Hand, the voice of the radio host, God, the bartender, inspector X, the healer, man on the cliff, the narrator on the Santa Monica pier.

Logline: Slave to my Bitchy Robottilla:
WALT’s roommates set up a date for him with a girl. WALT falls terribly in love with his abusive ROBOTTILLA. Later on she faints and forces WALT to find a new red blinking communication crystal, named “Heart-Stone”, to help her escape planet Earth.



Logline: Coke Master:
A syndicate has a technological advanced traffic-system to bring their cocaine or something to their customers.
Such a perfect operation is of course conducted by a very smart and shrewd leader, Rudolf Werner.
The U.S. in it's drive to exterminate all drugs everywhere, has invited a number of police-inspectors to get the right hands-on experience.

Paris did send one of their most motivated and disposable men, Jack Caruso.




Logline: Amsterdam Frozen Lover Captain:
A thriller roadmovie taking the viewer from Amsterdam to Budapest along the rivers Rhine and Danube by bicycle, train and boat, ending in a love triangle, that explodes.
